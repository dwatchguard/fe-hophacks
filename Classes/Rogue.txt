Rogue
Theme:
Knife wielding rogues who posses high speed, attack, and charisma. Good at dispatching single squishy units, but lack the defensive capabilities to survive large skirmishes
	Weapons: Knife
	Skill - Pass (move through enemy units)
	Class Change Options:
		Assassin
		Swashbuckler

Assassin
Theme:
A rogue who has gained access to a bow.
    Weapons: Knife, Bow
    Skill - Backstab extra damage if enemy is facing away
    Upgrade Skill - Pass go through an enemy, backstab on them does more
    Class Change Options:
        Whisper
        Kingslayer
Swashbuckler
Theme:
    Weapons: Knife, Sword
    Skill - Flanking if you and an ally are adj to enemy, extra damage
    Upgrade Skill - Pass go through an enemy, allies deal more
    Class Change Options:
        Rake
        Sparrow

Whisper
    Weapons: Knife, Bow
    Skill - Blinding Powder - Unit stealths for 3 turns.  Can take action after using. (Cooldown)
    Upgrade Skill - Backstab has SKL% to sleep target
    Capstone - Rewind - after a backstab, stealth for 1 turn and may warp to starting position  (Cooldown)
Kingkiller
    Weapons: Knife, Bow
    Skill - Blink Teleport behind target enemy within range (skill/4). Cannot otherwise move. Can take action afterwards(cooldown).
    Upgrade Skill - Backstab has extra dmg based on skill
    Capstone - Overstrike - Can take 1 additional action this turn. Next 2 turns cannot act. (Cooldown)
Rake
Theme: Bard like with an emphasis on tanking and buff/debuffs charming, yet dangerous
    Weapons: Knife, Sword
    Skill - Killing an enemy grants +3 SKL SPD ATK MAG to allies within
        two squares (decays over two turns; refreshes, doesn't stack)
    Upgrade Skill - allies get flanking bonus when you're adj to the enemy
    Capstone - Charm target unit unequips weapon. (cooldown)
Sparrow
Theme: Mobile controls own and enemy positions
    Weapons: Knife, Sword
    Skill - Lunge swap places with you and target before attack
			Disengage move backwards 2 after attack
    Upgrade Skill - you can flank for yourself if you start turn adj
    Capstone - Pull enemy back two spaces with you after attack (cooldown)
